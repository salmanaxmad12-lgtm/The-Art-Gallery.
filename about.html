About Our Gallery

Learn more about who we are and what makes us special.

Our Story

ArtGallery was founded in 2020 with one goal: making art accessible to everyone. What started as a small gallery in Mogadishu has become a welcoming place for artists and visitors from around the world.

We have hosted more than 50 exhibitions and worked with over 100 talented artists, helping local creativity reach an international audience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire creativity, support local artists, and connect people through art. We believe every artwork tells a story worth sharing.

Through exhibitions, workshops, and community events, we encourage young artists to discover and develop their talents.
